Threats We Fight

Understanding the dangers is the first step to protecting your child in the digital world.

🎮

In-Game Predators

Roblox, Minecraft, Fortnite — predators use in-game chat to groom children. We teach warning signs and how to configure every safety setting.

🤖

Dangerous AI Chatbots

Not all AI is safe. We identify which AI platforms pose risks to children and how bad actors use AI to generate harmful content and target kids.

💬

Messaging App Grooming

WhatsApp, Snapchat, Discord, Instagram — groomers exploit messaging apps daily. Learn the warning signs and monitoring tools available to parents.

🌐

Harmful Websites

Explicit content, radicalization, and self-harm communities. We provide DNS filtering guides, browser safety configs, and reporting tools.

📸

AI Deepfakes & Sextortion

AI-generated images of children are a growing global crisis. We educate families on reporting, prevention, legal resources, and emotional recovery.

🔐

Cybersecurity Gaps

Weak passwords, public WiFi, unsecured devices leave families vulnerable. Our step-by-step checklists close every gap — no tech knowledge needed.

Our Safety Programs

Practical, actionable programs designed for parents, children, and educators worldwide.

🎮 GAMING

Roblox Safety Lab

Step-by-step guide to locking down Roblox privacy settings, blocking strangers, reporting abuse, and understanding everything your child sees in-game.

🤖 ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

AI Threat Awareness

How to identify unsafe AI platforms, what questions to ask, which AI tools have genuine child safety measures, and how to talk to your kids about AI.

💬 MESSAGING

Grooming Watch Program

Warning signs of grooming behavior across Discord, Snapchat, WhatsApp, and Instagram. Includes conversation scripts for parents to use with their children.

📱 DEVICES

Amazon Kids & Parental Controls

Configure Amazon Kids, Google Family Link, Apple Screen Time, and Android Family controls for complete device protection at every age level.

🛡️ CYBERSECURITY

Family Cyber Defense

DNS filtering with Cloudflare for Families, VPN basics, password managers, and two-factor authentication — practical security for the whole family.

🎓 EDUCATION

Digital Citizenship Academy

Monthly video lessons for children ages 6–16 covering respectful online behavior, privacy awareness, and recognizing dangerous situations.
