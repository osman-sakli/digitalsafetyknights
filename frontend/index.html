Threats We Fight

Understanding the dangers is the first step to protecting your child in the digital world.

🎮

In-Game Predators

Roblox, Minecraft, Fortnite — predators use in-game chat to groom children. We teach warning signs and how to configure every safety setting.

🤖

Dangerous AI Chatbots

Not all AI is safe. We identify which AI platforms pose risks to children and how bad actors use AI to generate harmful content.

💬

Messaging App Grooming

WhatsApp, Snapchat, Discord, Instagram — groomers exploit messaging apps daily. Learn the warning signs and monitoring tools available.

🌐

Harmful Websites

Explicit content, radicalization, and self-harm communities. We provide DNS filtering guides and browser safety configs.

📸

AI Deepfakes & Sextortion

AI-generated images of children are a growing global crisis. We educate families on reporting, prevention, and legal resources.

🔐

Cybersecurity Gaps

Weak passwords, public WiFi, unsecured devices leave families vulnerable. Our step-by-step checklists close every gap.
